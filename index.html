Background

I came into IT through the business side — an MBA and a curiosity about how organisations think and where they hurt. I started as a Business Analyst at Hansen Technologies, where I spent years translating complex business needs into solutions for the utilities and energy sector. That grounding in product thinking led me to a Product Architect role, shaping how software evolved to meet real customer problems. Today I'm an Enterprise Architect at Eutelsat, working at the intersection of business strategy and technology — designing the systems and structures that keep a global satellite operator moving forward.

What sets my approach apart is where I started — on the business side. I don't just see technology as infrastructure; I see it as a response to a problem someone real is trying to solve. That lens has followed me from BA to Enterprise Architect, and it shapes every decision I make.
